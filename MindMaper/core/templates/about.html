Welcome to MindMapper

MindMapper is a smart personality and career assessment platform designed to help students and professionals make informed decisions about their future.

🌟 Our Vision

We envision a world where everyone has access to personalized career guidance. MindMapper bridges the gap between interest, skill, and opportunity through AI-powered assessments.

🧠 How It Works

  1. Basic Test: Determines broad career domains like Engineering, Medical, Law, Arts, etc.
  2. Advanced Test: Refines the recommendation to specific roles such as Data Scientist, Surgeon, Civil Engineer, etc.
  3. ML-Based Results: Your answers are analyzed by our trained machine learning model to give personalized recommendations.

  • ✔ Personalized result dashboards
  • ✔ Built-in light/dark mode for comfortable experience
